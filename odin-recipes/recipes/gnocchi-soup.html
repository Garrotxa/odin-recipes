Gnocchi Soup

Description

This is a soup my dad taught me to make. It's one of the first proper dishes I learned to cook for my family. The soup is made with chicken stock as the base. Added to it are various vegetables, spices, and meats. For my personal recipe, I use the ingredients listed below, but feel free to substitute as desired.

This soup is a healthy, delicious, easy-to-cook crowd-pleaser. It tastes great re-heated, and I love to make a large pot of it to eat multiple times in a week.

Ingredients

  • Two cartons of chicken stock
  • One pound of chicken, pan-fried and cut into chucnks.
